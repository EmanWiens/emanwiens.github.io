Particle System, Fall 2018

This is a particle system that I had to do for an assignent and then later slightly modified. It's a rather simple program, but I really enjoyed writing it. I also like the idea of using small particles to simulate things like smoke and fire.

The particles have mass and slightly change color on every collision.

3D free walk, Fall 2018

This is a program that I am particularly proud of. I had programmed many 2D animations but nothing in 3D. The reason was that I never knew where to start with it, and it was scary. This was an assignment I had to tackle in class and I enjoyed it much more than I thought I would.

This does not involve much more than being able to freely walk in a 3D space and some 3D animations. I also added collision detection to prevent the player from being able to walk through walls and exhibits (the floating pictures).
